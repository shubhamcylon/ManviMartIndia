Return & Refund Policy

We want you to be satisfied with your purchase. Please read our return policy carefully.

1. Return Eligibility

For products purchased through Flipkart, Amazon, Meesho, or any external marketplace link, returns and refunds are handled by the marketplace where the order was placed. Please check the return window and seller policy on that marketplace before buying.

2. Non-Returnable Items

Certain items cannot be returned, including:

  • Used or damaged products
  • Personal care products once opened
  • Items marked as non-returnable during sale

3. Refund Process

Refund approval, pickup, inspection and payment reversal timelines depend on the marketplace and seller policy shown during checkout.

Manvi Mart can help you find the correct support link, but the refund is processed by the marketplace where the purchase was completed.

4. Replacement

If the product received is defective, damaged, or incorrect, please raise a replacement or return request on the same marketplace where you placed the order.

5. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs for returning items may be the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to an error from our side (damaged or incorrect item).
